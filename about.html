About Us

Welcome to the Game Day Grill! Ever since our founder, Robert Clifton, was child he was inspired by his father's delectable cooking to open an honest, american restauant of his own. Robert finally opened Game Day Grill in 1987 and has been living the dream ever since! Our restauant serves the highest quality american food, such as burgers, brats, pulled pork, and more. Come for the food, stay for the vibrant atmosphere and wonderful staff!
